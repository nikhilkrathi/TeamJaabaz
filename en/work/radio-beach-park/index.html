Social Media

Music is contagious. And our work, too. To make Radio Beach Park's social media a success, we've revamped the digital brand persona, created interactive apps like "Nossa Playlist" and "What's Played", increased brand presence with creative content and created a serious relationship with the music's true lovers: the listeners and fans of the radio. The result is that from 2013 onwards we have increased by more than 1000% the number of followers.
