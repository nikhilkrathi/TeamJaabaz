Brand Activation

During the Easter period, we created a brand activation allowing the participants to race against each other by jumping higher and faster. To do this, we used 3D technology, Kinect, Flash, 2 jumpers and an LED screen with more than 4 meters. It was the first Brazilian's brand activation using Kinect technology.
